Morning Eggs

A healthy breakfast

Easy eggs to make in the morning and start your day. Plenty of protein and some greens!

Ingredients:

  • 3 Eggs
  • Chopped frozen spinach
  • Chik'n
  1. Thaw spinach in pan
  2. Warm up chik'n
  3. Crack eggs into pan
  4. Cook for 10 minutes at 250 degrees F
