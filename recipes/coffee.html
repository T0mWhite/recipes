Coffee

Get your head straight

Start your morning with a pick-me-up!

Tools & Ingredients:

  • Coffee beans
  • Water pot
  • Chemex
  • Coffee filter
  1. Heat full pot of water to 190 degrees F
  2. Grind coffee beans to fill 1/2 of filter
  3. Place filter in Chemex
  4. Put grounds in filter
  5. Slowly pour water over ground beans until empty
